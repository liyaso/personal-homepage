About Me

My name is Liya Sojan, and I am currently studying Computer Science at Georgia State University. Computer Science has been a subject that I have been interested in ever since I was a young kid. This interest first started when I would research and follow all the latest tech trends. Then in eighth grade, I joined robotics and became a programmer for my team by learning C++. Within my first year, my team competed in the World Championship, which made me more passionate and drove me to become better at what I do by developing my skills. Throughout my four years of college I have learned more programming languages (Java, Python, SQL, HTML, CSS, etc), learned how to be an efficient, teammate, leader, and communicate efficiently.
